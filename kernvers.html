Knowing your kernver

To find your kernver, press escape + refresh + power on your chromebook, then press tab. Look for kernver = 0x000000"randomnumber", the "random number" is your kernver. On the image on the right, you can press tab to and look in "kernver" at the last number to find your kernver -->>
